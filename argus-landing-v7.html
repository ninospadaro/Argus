Frequently asked questions

Everything you
need to know.

How is Argus different from Rogo or ChatGPT?+

Rogo and ChatGPT are reactive — you ask, they answer. Argus is proactive: it works overnight without prompting and delivers analysis before you know to ask. Beyond this, Argus covers 8 integrated workflows across the full investment lifecycle, is priced for mid-market funds (not bulge-bracket banks), and is built natively for European regulatory standards. Rogo costs €100–200K per year and serves US banks. Argus starts at €999 per month and serves European funds.

Can I trust AI-generated research for investment decisions?+

Every claim in every Argus output is cited to its primary source — management accounts, regulatory filings, exchange announcements, analyst reports. You can verify anything in seconds. Argus is designed to give you the full information picture, not to replace your judgement. Investment professionals who use Argus describe it as having a well-prepared analyst who read everything overnight and flagged what matters.

How does Argus know what matters for my portfolio?+

During onboarding, you configure your portfolio positions, watchlist, investment theses, and key risk factors. Argus maintains persistent memory of this context — including your entry/exit levels, thesis assumptions, and covenant thresholds. Every briefing and every research answer is filtered through this specific lens. The longer you use Argus, the more it understands what matters to you.

What European regulatory sources does Argus cover?+

Argus natively integrates AFM (Netherlands), BaFin (Germany), and AMF (France) filings with live alerts and document parsing. It also covers SFDR Article 6/8/9 classification monitoring, AIFMD reporting, KvK and pan-European company registries, and Euronext real-time exchange filings. Financial data is normalised across IFRS and HGB reporting standards. Document processing works in Dutch, German, French, and English.

How long does onboarding take?+

Configuration takes 20–30 minutes. You input your portfolio positions, watchlist companies, and key investment theses. Argus delivers its first morning briefing the following morning at 07:00. No IT involvement required, no enterprise procurement — you sign up, configure, and you are live the next day.

Is my portfolio data secure?+

Yes. Your portfolio configuration and research queries are never used to train any model. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, isolated per customer, and never shared across accounts. Argus is GDPR-compliant, EU-hosted, and designed for institutional financial professionals who handle confidential deal information daily.

Does the CIM Intelligence module work with any PDF format?+

Yes. Argus processes CIMs in any PDF format — including scanned documents, mixed text/image PDFs, and files with complex table structures. The Financial Model Analyser handles Excel files in any format including non-standard layouts. If the document structure is unusual, Argus flags uncertainty rather than returning potentially incorrect figures.
